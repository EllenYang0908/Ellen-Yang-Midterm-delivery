Brand Story

XSpace aims to provide the best murder mystery experience in Tokyo, Japan, blending a rich cultural heritage with an innovative twist on entertainment. Originated from China, the founders recognized the burgeoning fascination among young individuals for immersive, enigmatic experiences. With meticulous attention to detail, we craft intricately woven narratives, inviting participants to step into gripping scenarios that challenge their wit and intuition. Our dedication to authenticity, coupled with the allure of unraveling mysteries, has positioned XSpace as the foremost destination for those seeking thrilling and immersive social engagements in the heart of Tokyo.
